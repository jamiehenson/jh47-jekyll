Alright there, I'm Jamie. I'm a recent Computer Science Masters grad from the University of Bristol, and am currently working for Caring.com in San Mateo, CA. Living in and loving San Francisco.

I have experience with both the front-end and the back-end sides of the coin but I prefer the creativity that front-end web development provides - Haml, SASS and CoffeeScript are my daily bread. While less critical to my work, I have a lot of experience in musical programming - having built a visually-driven algorithmic composer and a laser pendulum harp.

When not trying to convince computers to do my bidding, I'm a keen (and at times semi-professional) musician specialising in jazz bass, though I play guitar, drums and piano as well. When I get time off I'm usually writing and recording some tune or other. I also love games, and spend a good amount of time both playing and developing them.
